Why EduTalk?

EduTalk takes a new view on Augmentative and Alternative Communication. Instead of forming sentences using menus of deeply embedded words, EduTalk allows the user to generate sentances by utilizing a list of customizble subject-verbs.

Each subject-verb has it's own customizable grid of words. Each word can be customized with an icon, picture, or special color.

What We Love To Do

Customizeable

Each word can be customized with a different icon, picture, or color. This helps distinguish words and allows for easier recollection.

Device Sync

Lose or break your communicate device? No worries. Every customization is saved in the cloud and sync'd to your next device on login.

Web Accessible

Don't have a tablet for the full experience of EduTalk? Use our web portal to get the full experience of the app, in a browser!

Friendly Design

No matter the age or skills, any user can pick up the process of using EduTalk to generate sentences.

Deployable

Want to deploy EduTalk to an entire classroom, group, or institute? EduTalk offers bulk licensing or individual licensing to support anyone!

Analytics

Want to view analytics for your EduTalk app? Using our administration portal, you're able to view analytics for how the app is used, popular words/sentence, and more!
